Welcome! I am Xinyi Xu, a Ph.D. candidate in the National Key Laboratory of Science and Technology on Communications at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), advised by Prof. Gang Feng (IEEE Fellow).

My main research interests lie in intelligent mobile networks and Edge Intelligence.
